GET TO UNDERSTAND AGRIQUEST

MISSION

Cultivating agricultural understanding through interactive play.

WHAT ARE WE SOLVING?

Agriculture is seen as boring, not relevant to youth's future.

Youth are disconnected from farming; it's seen as "dirty work."

CBC requires practical agriculture, but schools lack resources.

Few digital tools make agriculture fun or tech-driven.

OUR SOLUTIONS

A fun, interactive farming game tailored to CBC learners. It helps them explore agricultural practices in a hands-on, engaging way.

A smart assistant within the game provides advice on farming, like how to manage crops or respond to weather changes.

Learners engage in a farming game that helps them understand crop care, soil science, and agribusiness by doing rather than just reading.

The game content is designed around Kenyan crops, climate, and curriculum, ensuring relevance and relatability for local learners.
